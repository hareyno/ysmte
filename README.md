# YSMTE

> Know where your products come from.

YSMTE (You Should Meet The Employees) is a product transparency platform that helps consumers understand the supply chains behind the products they buy.

By scanning a product barcode, users can explore:

- Product information
- Manufacturers
- Supply chains
- Factory locations
- Company transparency
- Public certifications
- Sourcing information

## Mission

Every product has a story.

YSMTE helps people discover it.
